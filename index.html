Flappy Retro

Tap to keep the tiny bird flying through neon pipes.

0
Click or press Space to start. Don’t hit the pipes or the ground.

How to play

  • Tap or press Space to flap upwards.
  • Dodge the pipes — touching one ends the run.
  • Each pipe you pass scores a point.

Controls

  • Flap / Start: Mouse click or Space
  • Restart: Click or Space after Game Over
  • Close game: Esc or ✕

Greenfield Minesweeper

Green tiles, red flags. Classic minesweeper in retro colours.

Mines: 0 | Flags: 0
Right-click to place a red flag. Left-click to reveal. Reveal a mine and you lose.
Right-click / long-press to toggle flags (mobile: long-press a cell).

How to play

  • Set rows, columns, and number of mines, then start a new game.
  • Left-click a tile to reveal it.
  • Right-click to place or remove a red flag.
  • Numbers show how many mines touch that square.
  • Win by clearing all safe squares or correctly flagging all mines.

Controls

  • Reveal: Left-click
  • Flag: Right-click / long-press
  • New game: “New game” button
  • Reset board: “Reset board” button
  • Close game: Esc or ✕

2D Table Tennis

Local multiplayer pong-style table tennis with gravity bounces.

0 — 0
P1: W / S   |   P2: ↑ / ↓

How to play

  • Two players share one keyboard.
  • Ball must bounce on the table before a paddle can hit it.
  • If a paddle touches the ball before the first bounce, the other player scores.
  • Ball going past a paddle gives a point to the other side.

Controls

  • P1 Move: W / S
  • P2 Move: ↑ / ↓
  • Restart scores: “Restart Match” button
  • Close game: Esc or ✕

Mini Pac-Man

Navigate the maze, eat all the dots, and avoid the chasing monster.

Score: 0

Use the arrow keys to move Pac-Man. Eat all the dots and avoid the red monster.

How to play

  • Use the arrow keys to move Pac-Man around the maze.
  • Move over dots to eat them and increase your score.
  • If the monster touches you, the round ends and the maze resets.

Controls

  • Restart: “Restart” button
  • Close game: Esc or ✕

Neon Snake

Slither through a glowing grid, eat energy orbs, and don’t bite your own tail.

Score: 0

Use the arrow keys to move. Eat the glowing orbs to grow. Don’t hit the walls or yourself.

How to play

  • Use the arrow keys to steer the snake on the neon grid.
  • Move over glowing orbs to eat them and increase your score.
  • Each orb makes your snake longer and the game faster.
  • Hitting the wall or your own body ends the run.

Controls

  • Move: ↑ ↓ ← →
  • Restart: “Restart” button
  • Close game: Esc or ✕
